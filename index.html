I arrived to Mostly E-Bikes about a quarter before they closed, needing a tube/tire on my semi-new Trek road bike. George dropped what he was doing and immediately gave me and the bike his full attention. The bike had the original super thin racing/road tires. If I was gonna move around on it, this wasn’t going to be practical, let alone comfortable. He then made some suggestions on how to make some basic changes and keep them inexpensive. The bike was patched.
